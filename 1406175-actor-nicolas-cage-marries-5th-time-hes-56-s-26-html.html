Actor Nicolas Cage marries for 5th time, he's 56 and she's 26

Posted by Billy Koelling on Thursday, August 15, 2024

- Popular US actor Nicolas Cage has got married for a whopping fifth time

- The 56-year-old thespian tied the knot with Riko Shibata, 26, in Las Vegas in February

- Nicolas shared that they got engaged on FaceTime a few months back due to the coronavirus pandemic

Nicolas Cage has tied the knot for the fifth time. The popular actor reportedly got married to Riko Shibata in Las Vegas in February.

The 56-year-old superstar and Riko, 26, got hitched in Sin City's Wynn Hotel. For the wedding, Nicolas wore a Tom Ford tux while his bride rocked a handmade Japanese bridal kimono.

Nicolas reportedly proposed with a black diamond and mailed it to Riko, who was in Japan at the time.

According to E! News, Nicolas said they got engaged on FaceTime due to the coronavirus pandemic. Riko had left New York for Japan and Nicolas was in Nevada when he popped the question.
