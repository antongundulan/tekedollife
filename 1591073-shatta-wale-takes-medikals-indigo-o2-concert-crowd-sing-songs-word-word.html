Shatta Wale Takes Over At Medikal's Indigo O2 Concert, Crowd Go Gaga: &quot;He Stole The Show&quot;

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, August 28, 2024
  • Ghanaian musician Shatta Wale has been named as one of the top performers at Medikal's Indigo O2 concert
  • The Taking Over hitmaker got the crowd jamming all to his hit songs at the historic event on May 3, 2024
  • Some social media users have commented on Shatta Wale's video trending on Instagram as they shared their views

Ghanaian musician Charles Nii Armah Mensah Jnr, popularly called Shatta Wale, has earned the respect of many with his electrifying performances at Medikal's Indigo O2 concert.

The audience couldn't keep calm as Shatta Wale mounted the stage to perform his viral song 'Taking Over'.

Shatta Wale, who joined his friend at he event delighted the fans with his impressive dance moves while they sang his hit songs word for word in a viral video.
