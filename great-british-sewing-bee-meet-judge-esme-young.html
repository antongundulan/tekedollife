Posted by Tandra Barner on Saturday, August 10, 2024

Esme is an esteemed fashion designer

    The latest series of is well underway with the new batch of amateur sewers hoping to impress the judges and earn a place in the semi-final.

    But what do you know about judge Esme Young? Find out all about the diminutive and darling fashion designer here…

    Esme has been a judge on the beloved BBC programme since 2016 but when she's not presenting the show, the 75-year-old is a teacher at prestigious London art school, Central Saint Martins - which is where she learnt the ropes of fashion design herself.

    She began her career back in the 1970s, launching the designer collective Swanky Mode with three of her female friends, Judy Dewsbery, Melanie Langer and Jeanette Beckham, shortly after she graduated.
