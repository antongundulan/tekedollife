How Much Do Merry Maids Charge?

Merry Maids charges an hourly rate of $100 for two cleaners, with a two-hour minimum requirement. Many Merry Maid franchisees are now offering discounts ranging from $50 to $100 off your first five weekly or bi-weekly cleanings.

So, how much does it cost to have someone come in and clean your home in light of this?

The typical cost of home cleaning is $25-50 per hour per cleaner, per cleaning service provider. The overall cost is determined on the size of the residence and the kind of cleaning required. In the typical 3-bedroom, 2000 square foot house, cleaning costs $150 to $250, but a one-bedroom apartment costs $80 to $110 to clean on average.
