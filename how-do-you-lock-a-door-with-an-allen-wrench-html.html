How do you unlock a bathroom door from the outside?

To open a locked bathroom door from the outside, place the point of a butter knife into the keyhole if it’s a push button lock, and turn the knife sideways. If this doesn’t work, put a straightened out bobby pin into the lock. Then, turn the door knob and shake the pin, which should cause the lock to open.
