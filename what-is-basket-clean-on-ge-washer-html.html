What is basket clean on GE washer?

Posted by Billy Koelling on Friday, August 23, 2024

Answer

Once a month, according to GE Appliances, you should clean the washing tub in order to maintain the wash basket fresh and clean. Mold and mildew scents will be reduced as a result of this. Open the washer lid and pour one cup (or 250 mL) of liquid bleach, vinegar, or a washing machine cleanser into the basket. Close the top and close the washer. Close the lid and pick the Basket Clean cycle from the drop-down menu.

After all, what exactly is a “clean basket” on a washing machine?

The Basket Clean function is available on the majority of GE Appliance front-loading washers. This function thoroughly cleans the basket, removing any residue and odour. Every month, it is suggested that you take use of this function.

In addition, how does the self-cleaning washer function?

In order to perform a Self Clean cycle, first ensure that the washer is completely empty, then hit Self Clean and then Start. Self Clean (also known as Pure Cycle or Self Clean+) is an unique cycle that helps to avoid mould growth and is meant to clean sections of the tub where moisture and detergent residue may have accumulated but where a regular cycle would not be able to reach.
