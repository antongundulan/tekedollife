Sheree Whitfield Net Worth 2023: What Is The RHOA Star Worth?

Posted by Patria Henriques on Friday, August 23, 2024

Sheree Whitfield, from the reality TV show The Real Housewives of Atlanta (RHOA), has seen her net worth fluctuate over the years. As of 2023, her estimated net worth stands at a commendable $800,000, according to Celebrity Net Worth. This figure shows her entrepreneurial spirit and her ability to reinvent herself in the face of adversity.

Born Sheree M. Fuller on January 2, 1970, Whitfield's journey to fame began when she joined the cast of RHOA in 2008. The show catapulted her into the public eye and provided a platform to showcase her life, including the highs and lows of her divorce from NFL player Bob Whitfield. Her return to the series in its final two seasons was a strategic move by the show's executives, responding to fans' desire to see her back on screen.

Earnings From The Real Housewives of Atlanta

Whitfield's earnings from RHOA significantly contributed to her net worth. She is reportedly estimated to earn around $550,000 per season during her first four seasons. Her earnings escalated to $800,000 for season nine and reached a whopping $1 million for the show's tenth season. Further, Whitfield's divorce from Bob Whitfield was a significant event in her life, both personally and financially. She reportedly hoped for a seven-figure settlement but was denied alimony, receiving only child support payments. Legal issues also impacted her finances, with the IRS supposedly seeking $111,000 in back taxes at one point.

Entrepreneurial Ventures

ATLANTA, GEORGIA - NOVEMBER 28: Sheree Whitfield speaks onstage during Get Out To Vote Concert & Rally at Center Stage on November 28, 2021 in Atlanta, Georgia. (Photo by Paras Griffin/Getty Images)

Despite the challenges, Whitfield has shown an entrepreneurial spirit. She released a fictional novel, Wives, Fiancees, and Side-Chicks of Hotlanta, and launched her own clothing line, She By Sheree. Initially, the clothing line wasn't successful, and it took some time to get off the ground. However, Whitfield continued to press forward. She hosted a runway show on RHOA that was applauded by her peers after years of a lack of movement.
