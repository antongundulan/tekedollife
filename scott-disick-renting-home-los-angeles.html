Scott Disick puts his LA bachelor pad up for rent: see inside

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, August 17, 2024

The Keeping Up with the Kardashians star bought the property in 2016

    Scott Disick is renting out his mansion in the Hidden Hills area of Los Angeles after it failed to sell. The Keeping Up with the Kardashians star bought the property for $5.96million (£4.62million) in 2016, but decided to sell it less than a year later, listing it on the market for $8.8million (£6.83million).

    RELATED: See more celebrity homes here

    The father-of-three has now decided to rent the home, but it doesn't come cheap! According to Trulia, the recently renovated property will cost its tenants $60,000 (£46,500) per month to rent.

    For that price they will have full access to the seven-bedroom, seven-bathroom home, which spans 8,110 square feet. The property is private, and located at the end of a quiet cul-de-sac, with expansive grounds that have a large swimming pool, basketball area, and outdoor seating.

    Inside there is a new chef's kitchen and large family room, a downstairs children's wing and a separate guest suite that has its own entrance – perfect for a housekeeper or nanny. Click through the gallery to see inside Scott's home…

    Photo: Trulia

    The home has a bright, white interior, with wooden flooring throughout. There is an open plan design on the ground floor, with access directly from the living room and dining room out onto the large garden via glass patio doors.

    Photo: Trulia

    Scott's home is the perfect bachelor pad, with a large area downstairs for a pool table, where the reality TV star and his friends could have a games night. The room is decorated in a monochrome colour scheme, with striking black and white artwork lining one wall.

    Photo: Trulia

    The chef's kitchen was recently fitted and appears to be in pristine condition. Much like the rest of the property, the kitchen is decorated in all white, with marble counters and worktops. As well as offering plenty of storage space and room for kitchen, there is also a breakfast bar with seating for three people.

    Photo: Trulia

    The property has a large private back garden, where there is a terrace with outdoor sofa area for Scott's new tenants to relax on. Sun loungers lie on the grass by the swimming pool, and there is also an additional basketball area and fire pit.

    Photo: Trulia

    One of the standout features of the property is this wine cellar that is tucked under the stairs on the ground floor. This glass-doored storage room can be filled with hundreds of bottles – ideal for a party or for any wine connoisseurs to store their collection.

    Photo: Trulia

    The mansion has seven bathrooms, including this incredibly spacious master suite, which has a large sink and dressing table area, a new free-standing bath tub and tiled flooring.  

    Photo: Trulia

    One of the home's other major selling points is the huge walk-in wardrobe that leads off the master suite. Ideal for a celebrity, the room is lined with rail and drawer space, along with shelving to display Scott's shoe collection.

    Photo: Trulia
