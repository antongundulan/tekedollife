Amanda Holden's blue draped Zara blouse is a classic wardrobe staple

Posted by Kelle Repass on Friday, August 9, 2024

The Britain's Got Talent judge dazzles in her latest outfit...

    Amanda Holden looked so chic on Thursday morning as she shared her newest outfit she rocked to Heart Radio on Instagram. The ITV favourite kept it simple, leaving her skirts she has worn all week at home, instead donning a pair of tailored trousers by Markus Lupfer. But did you see her top? She decided to wear a fabulous Zara blue blouse, which had wide lapels, gathered cuffs and a lovely layered shape. It appears to be a past-season buy, and Amanda's Instagram fans were not impressed. Talking to the comments section, one wrote: "So frustrating when they wear something and you can’t buy it!" Another agreed, writing: "Love the shirt but can’t find it on Zara." And a third said: "The shirt is not online," followed by a sad face emoji. Don't worry though ladies, we've found a great alternative with shorter sleeves, for just £5.99 in the brand's sale. Result!

    Amanda donned a fab Zara blouse at Heart Radio HQ

    Amanda always wears high street clothes for her daily role, and her look is always put together by stylist Karl Willett.

    Get the look! £5.99, Zara

    The talented professional told HELLO! that he and Amanda often choose clothes that are readily available. "I would describe Amanda's style as fresh and elegant and she proves you can still look chic and be comfortable at the same time. Our purpose is to inspire every day woman to re-create these looks from the high street."

    The pair have a great working relationship, especially during the Britain's Got Talent live shows where the blonde beauty is known for her stunning, Avant Garde dresses. "Amanda is a dream to work with," he revealed. " She's a dream client, and a friend! She loves to take risks and be adventurous. Her approach to fashion and style is exciting for me - she is open to experiment and push the boundaries of previous years."
