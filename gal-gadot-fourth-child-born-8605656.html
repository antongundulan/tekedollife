Gal Gadot Welcomed Her Fourth Child

Posted by Tandra Barner on Tuesday, August 20, 2024

Gal Gadot just made her husband Jaron Varsano the definition of a girl-dad by welcoming her fourth daughter. On Wednesday, the Wonder Woman actress announced the exciting news on Instagram with a sweet photo of her holding the newest addition to their family: Ori.

In the image, the baby girl is swaddled in a blanket with a striped hat on her head while lying on Gadot's chest. The actress, who is sitting in a hospital bed, wrapped her arms around the baby and pressed her face against the little one's head.
