Lady Amelia Spencer celebrates bachelorette party in the most glamorous dresses

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, August 4, 2024

The bash gave us plenty of Hen Do inspo…

    In what’s sure to be the society wedding of the year, the nuptials of Lady Amelia Spencer are imminent, which can only mean one thing… an epic hen do. (Or as the half-South African bride-to-be refers to it, a bachelorette party.) 

    The aristocratic daughter of Charles Edward Maurice Spencer, 9th Earl Spencer took to Instagram to show off her celebrations. The niece of Princess Diana is due to wed her long-term partner Greg Mallett in the new year and she is now stirring up wedding day fever courtesy of her friends and twin sister Lady Eliza Spencer.

    MORE: Lady Amelia and Eliza Spencer outdo themselves in cut-out Carolina Herrera gowns for glittering bash

    RELATED: Lady Amelia and Eliza Spencer twin in shimmering mini dresses for exclusive night out

    The socialite shared a series of Instagram stories of the day, which showed the spectacular celebrations. Lady Amelia, 30, attended her ‘bachelorette party’ alongside her sister, wearing a stunning silver cut out mini dress paired with gold chain effect stiletto sandals. She also had an outfit change into a white dress featuring gentle puff sleeves and a plunging neckline. 

    The sister of Dolce & Gabbana ambassador Lady Kitty Spencer posed in front of a large balloon arch, made up of pink, gold and white balloons, as well as metallic pink letter balloons which spelled out ‘Bride To Be.’ Romantic pink was the order of the day, with her hens all wearing various shades of the rosy hue, including her sister who wore a short floral white dress printed with an array of pink flowers. 

    MORE: Lady Amelia and Eliza Spencer amp up the glamour in designer eveningwear

    RELATED: Lady Amelia and Eliza Spencer channel Princess Diana in retro jeans and winter knits

    It seems like everyone got the Barbiecore memo, with a welcome sign which said “Welcome to Amelia’s Bachelorette” printed in pink writing surrounded by a pink floral motif. 

    Lady Amelia has been enjoying an active wedding season, and recently attended a friend's wedding ceremony alongside her fiancée, looking divine in baby pink. The socialite slipped into a Versace dress featuring gentle puff sleeves, a V-neckline, an ankle-length cut, a sweetheart silhouette and a romantic wrap effect for the occasion.
