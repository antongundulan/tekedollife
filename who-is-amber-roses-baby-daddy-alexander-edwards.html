Who Is Cher's boyfriend Alexander Edwards?

Posted by Billy Koelling on Friday, August 23, 2024

MUSIC executive, Alexander Edwards, is famously known for being one of Amber Rose's baby daddies.

However, since November 2022, the 37-year-old has been linked to iconic singer, Cher, and things seem to be getting serious.

Who Is Cher's boyfriend Alexander Edwards?

Alexander "AE" Edwards is a rapper and music businessman.

The Universal Music Group executive works with the likes of Post Malone, Billie Eilish and Ariana Grande.

AE is also signed to Tyga's label, Last Kings, and has a net worth of $2million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

He shares one child with model, rapper and television personality, Amber Rose.

When did AE and Cher start dating?

AE and Cher reportedly met during Paris Fashion Week in September 2022.

By November, the pair were spotted holding hands outside Los Angeles hot spots, Craig's and The Nice Guy.

In response to criticism regarding their 40-year age difference, Cher told Kelly Clarkson on her daytime talk show: "On paper, it's kind of ridiculous.

"But in real life, we get along great. He's fabulous. And I don't give men qualities that they don't deserve."

Cher is definitely not opposed to dating younger men, and in 2021, she told People: "The truth was if I hadn't gone out with younger men, I would have never had a date.

"Younger men weren't intimidated by older women. But older men in my age category, they weren't having it."

And while the pair aren't talking marriage just yet, Edwards wasn't shy about buying his girlfriend a diamond ring for Christmas.

Cher is so smitten, she told E! News in March 2023, that the couple would be collaborating on her next two albums.

She said: "I'm going to England to make two albums.

"Some of the songs Alexander gave me, so I'm pretty excited about that.

"He's a producer and a writer and he does everything, so I'm happy about that."

When was AE in a relationship with Amber Rose?

The pair first started dating in 2018, making their debut as a couple at her annual SlutWalk event.

In January 2019, Amber Rose claimed that Edwards came into her life at a time "where I've had enough of the narcissistic, cheating and abuse I've endured over the years from my previous relationships".

Later that year, in October, Rose and Edwards welcomed their first child together - a son, Slash.

The 39-year-old rapper had her first son, Sebastian, in 2013, when she was dating rapper, Wiz Khalifa.

However, on August 18, 2021, Amber accused AE of cheating on her with 12 separate women.

She took to Instagram to slam her baby daddy, writing: "I'm tired of getting cheated on and being embarrassed behind the scenes.

"All 12 of y'all bums (the ones that I know of there's probably more) can have him.

"Y'all very much knew he was in a relationship with a baby and y'all decided to f*** him anyway.
