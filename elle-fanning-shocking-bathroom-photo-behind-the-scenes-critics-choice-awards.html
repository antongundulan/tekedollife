Elle Fanning shares surprising bathroom photo from the Critics Choice Awards

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, August 9, 2024

Her most candid photo yet

    Elle Fanning sure knows how to get pulses racing! The star shocked fans after sharing a very cheeky backstage photo.

    MORE: Elle Fanning shares never-before-seen pictures in heartfelt tribute to sister Dakota

    On Sunday night, the actress attended the Critics Choice Awards, for which she wore a stunning gold sequin strapless Oscar de la Renta gown fit for a princess.

    She however proved every bit of award show prep isn't always glamorous, taking fans behind-the-scenes as she tried to go to the bathroom in her voluminous gown.

    WATCH: Elle shared big news about the future of her hit Hulu show

    MORE: Elle Fanning leaves fans shocked by her appearance for new true crime role

    The actress shared a hilarious photo of herself leaning back on a toilet with her toned legs lifted as she held on to a railing and flew her arm up. Fans were left not knowing where to look, either the stunning gown, the toilet paper in Elle's hand or most surprising of all, the nude thong stretched between her knees.

    Actress Joey King noted the chaotic features of the candid moment, commenting: "Tag yourself. I'm the toilet seat cover dispenser."

    Elle fittingly captioned the photo with: "The Golden Throne," alluding to her glittery monochrome look.

    Elle's dazzling ballerina-like look
