The Usos Finally Reach Unbelievable WWE Milestone

Posted by Tandra Barner on Tuesday, August 20, 2024

The Usos defeated American Alpha to win the SmackDown Tag Team Championship on the March 21st, 2017, episode of WWE SmackDown. They were the first and only team to win both the Raw and SmackDown Tag Team Championships. It’s 2022, and now they’ve officially become the WWE tag team champions with the longest reign.

On Monday, November 14th, The Usos became the company’s longest-reigning WWE Tag Team Champions. The Usos have held the WWE SmackDown Tag Team Titles for 484 days. They surpassed the WWE Raw Tag Team Title reign of The New Day, which lasted 483 days from August 23rd, 2015 to December 18th, 2016.

On July 18th, 2021, The Usos defeated The Mysterios to win the WWE Raw Tag Team Championships at WWE Money in the Bank. Along with becoming the longest-reigning WWE SmackDown Tag Team Champions, the duo also won the WWE Raw Tag Team Championships, making them the Undisputed WWE Tag Team Champions. On the May 20th episode of WWE SmackDown, they defeated RK-Bro.

During their reign, the Usos successfully defended the titles 16 times. They were able to extend their reign and break the record on Friday’s WWE SmackDown by defeating New Day. To get the latest updates, keep an eye on Ringside News.
