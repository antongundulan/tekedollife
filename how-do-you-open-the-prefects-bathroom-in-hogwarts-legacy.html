How do you open the prefects bathroom in Hogwarts Legacy?

Posted by Tandra Barner on Thursday, August 8, 2024

To open the prefects bathroom in Hogwarts Legacy, you need to follow these steps:
1. Head to the Faculty Tower in the Hospital Wing located off the Grand Staircase’s South Wing.
2. Unlock the Alohomora spell to bypass locked doors. This spell can be gained after completing The First Trial quest.
3. Once you have the Alohomora spell, use it to unlock the locked doors leading to the prefects bathroom.
4. Enter the unlocked bathroom and explore the area.

How do you get to the locked toilets in Hogwarts Legacy?

To get to the locked toilets, you need to:
– Travel to the Faculty Tower in the South Wing.
– Head down the stairs to the ground floor of Gryffindor Tower.
– Move through the hall until you reach the locked bathroom where Peeves causes chaos.
– Use the Alohomora spell to unlock the door and gain access to the locked toilets.

What is the password to the Prefects bathroom?

The current password to the Prefects bathroom is “Pine-fresh”. This password is required to enter the bathroom and access its facilities.

Who can use the Prefects bathroom?

The Prefects’ bathroom is a special bathroom on the fifth floor of Hogwarts that is restricted to use by school Prefects, Head Boys, Head Girls, and Quidditch captains. Only those who hold these positions or are given the correct password can access the bathroom.

How do you get to Moaning Myrtle’s bathroom in Hogwarts Legacy?

To get to Moaning Myrtle’s bathroom in Hogwarts Legacy, you need to follow these steps:
– Proceed to The Greenhouses and locate the area right underneath Ravenclaw Tower.
– From the fast travel point at The Greenhouses, turn around and go through the big doorway into the castle.
– Continue to the corridor and head towards the end until you reach the entrance of Moaning Myrtle’s bathroom.
