&quot;Confusing Story Line, Very Poor&quot;: FansTackle Itele Over Netflix Movie, Kesari, Voice Note Leaks

Posted by Billy Koelling on Saturday, August 24, 2024
  • Nollywood actor and producer, Itele, faced the wrath of some movie lovers after they watched his film, Kesari
  • The moviemaker had put the film on Netflix for his fans to watch, but they were not satisfied with the story line
  • In a voice note sighted online, some fans said he shouldn't have put it on such a big platform as Netflix

Nollywood actor Ibrahim Yekini, professionally known as Itele, has come under intense criticism over his Netflix movie, Kesari.

The moviemaker had put the film on the streaming platform, but some fans were not satisfied with the project after watching it.

Critics of the project claimed the story line was very poor and also queried the person who wrote the movie's script.

Fans advise Itele

Dishing advice to the movie icon, some fans said the film should be recorded on CDs and sold instead of making it to Netflix.
