Liam Hemsworth bio

What is Liam Hemsworth age? When was he born? You have probably asked yourself these questions about the celebrity in a bid to know him better. The actor was born on January 13, 1990, in Melbourne, Australia to Craig and Leonie. He is the youngest in his family with two brothers, Luke, and Chris, who are also renowned actors.

The Hemsworth family moved and settled in Phillip Island, a small island in Australia when Liam was eight years old. Regarding his education, the last born in the family graduated from Lion High School and Newhaven College.

The Hemsworth brothers

The three siblings are not new to film fanatics. They are one of the most famous dynasties in the film industry and are widely known for their exceptional talents. From Luke to Chris and Liam, the three brothers have starred in numerous action and thriller movies and TV series.

Despite following the path of his siblings, Chis Hemsworth brother, Liam, did not find it easy to make a breakthrough in film. He first worked as a construction worker for several months and would later move to the United States to pursue a career in acting back in 2009. He and Chris stayed with William Ward, Chris' manager, for some time before moving to an apartment they rented in Los Angeles.

Liam's film debut came in 2009 where he secured a role in American-British science fiction disaster film, Knowing that starred Nicolas Cage. He later went on to star in the American movie, The Last Song (2010).

One of his most significant roles came in 2012 when he starred as Gale Hawthorne in the first of The Hunger Games film series. Other popular films by the actor include Paranoia (2013), The Expendables 2 (2012), and Isn't It Romantic (2019). He has also appeared in some TV shows like Jimmy Kimmel Live (2010-2015) andThe Muppets (2015) among others.

Miley Cyrus and Liam Hemsworth

The film star has probably grabbed more headlines than his elder brothers in the past few years, in terms of his love life, with Liam's on and off relationship with Miley Cyrus getting more limelight. The couple first met back in 2009 and enjoyed their quality time together for just five months. They later reconciled and were seen in various events together. The two announced their engagement in June 2012. Miley Cyrus Liam Hemsworth love story took another twist with the pair separating in September 2013.

The love birds were back together in 2016 and reportedly got married later in December 2018. When fans thought the two had settled, Liam Hemsworth Miley Cyrus marriage could not hold on for long, and the couple divorced in August 2019.

On his Instagram account, the actor wished Cyrus the best in her future. The split also made him cancel some of his promotional plans for his new action, crime film, Killerman (2019). In the movie, the actor stars as Moe Diamond, a New York City money launderer.
