U No Respect Ur Age: Paul Okoye Blasts Eedris Abdulkareem for Saying Psquare Attended 2004 Con

Posted by Kelle Repass on Tuesday, August 27, 2024
  • Paul Okoye has reacted to the interview granted by his senior colleague Eedris Abdulkareem over a concert in 2004
  • Abdulkareem had claimed that Psquare was part of the singers who were beaten at the backstage during the event
  • Responding to the claim, Okoye blasted Abdulkareem and maintained Psquare was not invited to perform at the concert

Paul Okoye of Psquare has called out his senior colleague, Eedris Abdulkareem, over his recent interview about a concert in 2004.

Legit.ng had reported that Abdulkareem claimed the Nigerian Breweries staged a concert in 2004 and invited American singer, 50 Cent.

He also alleged that some Nigerian artists were beaten at the backstage during the event. He noted that Psquare was part of the singers who were dealt with.

But reacting to the interview, Okoye said Abdulkareem was Iying. He added that Psqaure was not one of the singers invited to the concert.

Okoye takes a swipe at Abdulkareem

In the video, Okoye abused the rapper, who had kidney transplant a few years ago. He said they should have beaten him better than they did that day.

He stated he couldn't fathom that an old man like him would be saying such things.

Okoye asked why Abdulkareem would mention Psquare in his interview.

Okoye says Psquare was the Gen Z then

In his clip, Paul Okoye claimed that Psquare was still young at the time. He mentioned that they were the G Zen of the music industry during that era.

The singer praised upcoming music artistes for surviving on their own without any help.
