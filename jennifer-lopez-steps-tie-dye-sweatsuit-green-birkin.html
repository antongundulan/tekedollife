Jennifer Lopez Steps Out in Tie-Dye Sweat Suit and Another Birkin

Posted by Tandra Barner on Sunday, August 25, 2024

Another day, another peek at Jennifer Lopez's exclusive Birkin collection.

Yesterday, the multi-hyphenate was spotted heading to her dance studio in Los Angeles in a colorful and comfy look—accessorizing, of course, with a rare Hermès handbag from her luxe closet.

The Marry Me star's sporty outfit consisted of a rainbow tie-dye sweat suit, which she paired with white Nike sneakers with bright yellow and pink accents, hot pink sunglasses, and large silver hoop earrings. She also carried a forest green Birkin with gold hardware, and wore a dazzling diamond ring on her right hand and her wedding band on her left.

Bellocqimages/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

This is not the first time J.Lo has used a Birkin as her gym bag. Just last month, the stylish Mrs. Affleck headed to her dance studio wearing a pair of skintight black leggings in a metallic crocodile-skin print, a long-sleeved Kith Sport crop top with a faux turtleneck, Nike sneakers, and carrying a rare white-and-gold Hermès Birkin bag.

Bellocqimages/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

Weeks before that, she headed to dance rehearsal in a black zip-up cropped turtleneck, matching leggings by Thirty Years, white Nike Air Jordans with gold logos, and a black Hermès Birkin with gold hardware. She also accessorized with a pair of Versace shield sunglasses, with the lenses tinted to a dark orange-and-purple ombré.

And during the COVID-19 pandemic in December 2020, Lopez was seen working out at Alex Rodriguez's UFC Gym in Miami (when they were still together) in a maroon cropped sweatshirt and matching metallic leggings featuring sheer panels, which she paired with her Himalayan crocodile Birkin.
