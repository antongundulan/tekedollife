What is CFC charge on car rental?

Posted by Patria Henriques on Sunday, August 25, 2024
Spread the love             

Answer

The customer facility charge (CFC) is a fee levied by an airport operator on each rental car user and collected by rental car companies. CFCs are regulated at the state level rather than the federal level. As a result, authorization, collection, and project eligibility differ from one state to the next.
