How Big Is Papa John's Xl New York Style Pizza

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, August 11, 2024
People are currently reading this guide.

The Papa Johns XL New York Style Pizza: How Big Are We Talking? Spoiler Alert: It's YUGE!

Let's face it, folks, figuring out pizza sizes can be a confusing mess. You've got your smalls, your mediums, your larges... and then Papa Johns throws a whole new wrench into the works with their XL New York Style Pizza.

Is it just a regular pizza in disguise? Or is it a behemoth capable of feeding a small army (or a particularly hungry Netflix binge-watching session)? Buckle up, pizza pilgrims, because we're about to unveil the truth!

XL New York Style Pizza: By the Numbers

Here's the juicy info you crave: The Papa Johns XL New York Style Pizza boasts a diameter of a whopping 15.5 inches. That's right, folks, we're talking about a pizza that could double as a frisbee (though we wouldn't recommend it – toppings tend to fly in unexpected directions).

But Diameter Doesn't Tell the Whole Story...

Now, you might be thinking, "15.5 inches? That sounds impressive, but is it really that different from a regular large pizza?" Well, hold on to your pepperoni cups (because yes, Papa Johns offers those on this pizza!), because the magic of the XL New York Style lies in its oversized, foldable slices.

We're talking slices that won't leave you hanging halfway through a fold – perfect for New York-style pizza purists (or anyone who just appreciates a slice that holds its own).

So, How Big is This Pizza REALLY?

Imagine a pizza that can feed a family of four... with leftovers. Picture a pizza that could satisfy your post-marathon hunger pangs. Envision a pizza so big, you might need a friend (or two) to help you conquer it. That, my friends, is the Papa Johns XL New York Style Pizza.

Still Unsure? Here's a Handy Analogy:

Think of a regular large pizza as a frisbee you wouldn't throw. The XL New York Style Pizza? That's the frisbee you'd actually use (again, with caution!).

Papa Johns XL New York Style Pizza: The FAQ

Alright, alright, we know you have questions. Here are some quick answers to the most burning inquiries about this pizza giant:

How to Order the XL New York Style Pizza?

Head over to Papa Johns' website or app and look for the "New York Style Pizza Delivery" section. You can't miss it!

How Many Slices Does the XL New York Style Pizza Have?

This bad boy comes with a glorious 8 oversized slices.

How Much Does the XL New York Style Pizza Cost?

Prices may vary depending on location, but you can expect to pay a bit more than a regular large pizza.

How Long Does it Take to Cook the XL New York Style Pizza?

Cooking times can vary, but expect to wait a few minutes longer than a regular pizza. Patience is a virtue, especially when it comes to pizza this magnificent.

How to Eat the XL New York Style Pizza (Without Looking Like a Savage)?

Use a fork and knife... just kidding! Embrace the fold, my friend. That's what those oversized slices were made for!

So there you have it, folks! The Papa Johns XL New York Style Pizza: a pizza so big, it deserves its own zip code. Now go forth and conquer that pizza mountain (responsibly, of course).
