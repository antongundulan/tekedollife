What is the North South East West symbol called?

Posted by Billy Koelling on Sunday, August 4, 2024

Answer

It is a representation of the cardinal directions (north, east, south, and west), as well as their intermediate points, that is seen on a compass or other navigational instrument such as a map, nautical chart, or monument. A compass rose is also known as a windrose or rose of the winds.

Names have a Germanic origin.
