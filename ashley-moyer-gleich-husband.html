Career

Ashley Moyer-Gleich's illustrious career trajectory mirrors her diverse interests and multifaceted skills. While she stands in the spotlight today as a distinguished National Basketball Association referee, her journey has seen varied roles, each contributing to her growth and expertise.

Kickstarting her professional endeavors at Harpel’s Photography, Ashley took on the responsibilities of an Assistant Manager between 2009 and 2011. A brief stint in the legal domain saw her contributing as a Legal Assistant at Engler Law Office. Her scientific inclinations were evident when she worked as a Phlebotomist at Good Samaritan Health System, a role that aligned with her Phlebotomy Technician certification from Harrisburg Area Community College in 2012.

Basketball, however, was a consistent theme. Her involvement with Millersville University Women’s Basketball Team, first as a Graduate Assistant and later, her tenure as a Women’s Collegiate Basketball Official for the NCAA, attests to her commitment to the sport. This devotion found its pinnacle when she joined the WNBA as a referee and soon after, the NBA G-League, eventually solidifying her position as a full-time NBA referee.
